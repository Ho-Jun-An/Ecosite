Sustainability in our generation: why is it important?

Sustainability is the development/sourcing of goods, products and services to meet our current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs. This takes into consideration that the natural resources around us are finite and important that we use such resources rationally. In addition, sustainability also extends to the workplace; ensuring that companies provide workers with appropriate working conditions, job quality, earnings and work-life balance.
